PhotoPizza DIY

360° PRODUCT PHOTOGRAPHY TURNTABLE

PhotoPizza - is an open source project of the turntable for taking pictures of objects from all directions (3D-photo-360 spin-photo). The control unit platform is based on the Arduino. You can self-assemble the device from the available components, using the detailed instructions, and you do not need deep knowledge in electronics.
